SOIL


The unconsolidated mineral or organic material on the immediate surface of the Earth that serves as a natural medium for the growth of land plants. For a garden, the suitable type of soil is loam- a well-draining mix of sand, silt, and clay that provides
good aecration and retains moisture well, making it ideal for a variety of plants while still being manageablein a smaller space.
