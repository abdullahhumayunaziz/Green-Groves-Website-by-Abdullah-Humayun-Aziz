TIPS ON CHOOSING POTS

Use the rootball as a guide when choosing a pot for a single specimen plant.

Size, shape and drainage are critical factors in the selection of the container that will provide the best environment for growth of plants.

Choose all the same colour pot and different plants or all the same plant in different coloured pots.

POTS PLATES

Round plates or disks with indentations,slots,or holes evenly spaced aroundtheir outer edge.

Plates facilitates ventilation and absorption for your plants and allow air to enter the plants soil from outside because of their porosity.

Plants saucers can be a valuable addition to your planter for many reasons.

POTS HANGERS

Pots hanger allows to spend time admiring your handiwork & less time taking care of plant problem.

A decorative hanging plant support that is made by kotting fabric or cords together.

Pots hanging baskets provide vaaluable color at eye contant.

WALL-MOUNTED BRACKETS

A small container,typically the size of flower pot,that is mounted or hang on a wall.

Hanging plants may be in a baskets,planter boxes affixed to the wall,in pots that are tucked into cracks,or artfully planted on the top of the wall.

Instead of being grown in soil in a pot,the way most people grow their plants,these guys are mounted on a surface.

RAILING BRACKETS

Railing brackets are used at the end of a railing section, providing the necessary support and termination of the system.

Brackets can be used to support vertical garden systems allowing gardeners to grow herbs or flowers .

Many railing brackets are designed to withstand outdoor elements such as rain, sun, and snow, making them perfect for gardening use.
