We Exist To Help You Grow.

Our journey began like yours probably did - the simple desire to grow a few plants. Those early
grows opened our eyes to a different approach to life: growing our own food, flowers, and
cultivating a beautiful space to live in.

But as our experience grew in the garden, we realized that “gardening” was…kind of hard to
learn. On top of that, a majority of the products on the market fell short of our expectations on
quality, ease of use, and durability.

First, we started sharing. Everything. Our gardening successes, failures (there were a lot of
them), and educational tips. Over the years, over 12+ million aspiring gardeners have come to
trust us for our gardening education on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and more.

Next, we turned our focus to making gardening products that are actually designed by
gardeners. Every product we stock in our store is hand-selected after detailed research, testing,
and seasons worth of use in our own gardens. If it doesn't meet our standards, we don’t carry
it in our store... plain and simple.

From the sturdiest seed starting supplies, to meticulously crafted raised beds, to 650+ varieties
of non-GMO veggies, herbs, and flowers, we ensure our products “just work” for you and your
garden.

Because while gardening itself is messy and full of learning experiences, the products you use
to garden shouldn’t be.
